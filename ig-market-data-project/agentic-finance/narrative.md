# agentic-finance — Agentic Trading Systems

## Objective
Build autonomous trading agents that combine LLM reasoning (GPT-4o-mini)
with quantitative signals. Agents analyse market conditions, generate
trade ideas, manage risk, and explain their decisions — all auditable.

## Projects (Planned)
1. **Research Agent** — Fetches data, runs models, generates reports via LLM
2. **Execution Agent** — Reads signals, sizes positions, submits orders
3. **Risk Manager** — Monitors exposure, VaR, drawdown limits
4. **Portfolio Agent** — Allocates capital across strategies
5. **Audit Trail** — Full decision logging with LLM explanations
